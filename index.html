Welcome to Minesweeper! Left click to dig up a square. If it isn't a bomb, it'll be a yellow color. Right click to flag a square as a bomb.

What do the numbers mean? Well, if there's a 1, there's 1 bomb around that square. If there's an 8, there are 8 bombs around that square.
